## Operator Aritmatika
> +, - , * , / , %

```PHP
$x = 10;
$y = 2;
echo $x + $y;
```

## Concatenation
> .

```PHP
$nama_depan = "Yoga";
$nama_belakang = "QF";
echo $nama_depan . " " . $nama_belakang;
```
